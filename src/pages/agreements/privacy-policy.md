# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 30, 2025

---

## Personal Statement

**DigiTEC** is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data within our fund management system. As members of the East CDO Timbalo Eagles Club (ECTEC), your trust is important to us, and we strive to maintain transparency in all our data practices.

---

## What Information Do We Gather?

We collect the following information to operate the **DigiTEC** system:

### Account Information
- Name, email address, contact number, birthday, home address, employment information, membership details, and other personal information necessary for membership records.

### Financial Data
- Share capital contributions, club fund contributions, club expenses, loan applications, loan balances, and payment history.

### Technical Data
- Login credentials (securely hashed), session tokens, and activity logs for security and auditing purposes.

### User-Generated Content
- Profile photos, organization photos displayed on the landing page, announcements from board officers, and optional remarks on transactions.

> **Note:** All information is collected with your knowledge and is necessary for managing ECTEC's financial operations.

## Data Security

We implement multiple security measures to protect your information:

- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Users can only access data appropriate to their role (Admin, Treasurer, Board of Directors, or Member).
- **Encrypted Connections:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.
- **Activity Logging:** All system actions are recorded for accountability and audit purposes.
- **Supabase Security:** Our backend provider implements industry-standard security practices.

> **Important:** While we employ reasonable security measures, DigiTEC is currently a beta system. It has not undergone full production-level security audits and should not be considered to have enterprise-grade security.

## Your Rights

As a DigiTEC user, you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal and financial data stored in the system.
- **Request corrections** to inaccurate information through your designated treasurer or any ECTEC officer.
- **View** your transaction history at any time through your account.
- **Update or remove** your profile photo and personal information directly through your profile page.
- **Request account deactivation** by contacting the system administrator.

## Disclosure Of Your Information

We may disclose your information only in the following circumstances:

**Legal Requirements**  
When required by law, court order, or government regulation.

**Protection of Rights**  
To protect the rights, property, or safety of ECTEC, its members, or others.

**With Your Consent**  
When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information.

**Authorized Personnel**  
To ECTEC officers (Treasurer, Board of Directors) as necessary for their official duties, in accordance with role-based permissions.

---

## Data Retention

Financial records, including contributions, loans, and transactions, are **retained indefinitely** to maintain accurate, historical records and comply with cooperative financial practices. Even when transactions are marked as deleted, the data remains in the database for auditing purposes.

User accounts marked as **"Inactive"** or **"Revoked"** are retained in the system but access is restricted.
